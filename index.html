Recording equipment suggestions

Before buying any recording equipment, it's a good idea to look around for reviews and recommendations. If possible, try out the equipment before you buy it. There are too many options to get into detail, but I'll recommend 2 microphones to consider.

Audio Technica ATR2100-USB - This dynamic USB microphone is great if you are recording one person at a time. Because it's a dynamic mic, it won't pick up as much background noise as the more common condenser microphones. You can often find them for around $100.

Blue Yeti - A popular condenser microphone with multiple pickup patterns. If you are recording more than one person at a time, this is a decent option. They typically cost around $200.

Podcast Directories

You want people to be able to find your podcast and listen to it. There are a lot of podcast directories out there, but the ones listed here are the most popular.

Apple Podcasts - The biggest and most important podcast directory. Many podcast apps pull their lists from here.
